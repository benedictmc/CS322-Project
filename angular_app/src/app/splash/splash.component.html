Music Lyric Generator

Create new and unquie song lyrics based on your favorite artists. Made with textgenrnn and keras as a part of CS322 Maynooth University Music Programming Module

Utilises a recurrent neural network to and trains on 100000+ samples of an artists corpus of lyrics.

Choose Artist Dog Breed

Choose from a catalog of pre train artists models to generate lyrics for. Theses have been selected to showcase a broad range of lyrical structures and music genres.

Generate new lyrics Dog Breed

Jump and let the model generate a new set of lyrics. You have the option of automatically doing this or you can have the choice of input a sample of lyrics to act as a seed for the generateion.

Play new music Dog Breed

Though Google's text to speech API we are able to bring the music to life. Click play and hear the new creation of lyrics that have been generated through the web interface.
